03 FAQ
How long does delivery take? +

Standard delivery across Israel takes 1–3 business days. Express same-day delivery is available in Tel Aviv, Netanya, and Haifa for orders placed before 12:00 PM.

How do I get free shipping? +

All orders over ₪350 automatically qualify for free standard shipping. No code needed — the discount applies at checkout.

Do you ship outside Israel? +

Currently we ship within Israel only. International shipping is coming soon — follow us on Instagram for updates.

How will I know my order shipped? +

You'll receive a WhatsApp message with your tracking link as soon as your order leaves us. You can also DM us on Instagram anytime for a status update.

Can I return or exchange an item? +

Yes — within 7 days of delivery, items must be unworn and in original packaging. Message us on Instagram to start the process and we'll sort it out quickly.

What if my item arrives damaged? +

Take a photo immediately and send it to us on Instagram. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund — no questions asked.
